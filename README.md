# CS417-Juicy-Sim
Starting with nothing but a steady drip of Water, you’ll unlock and automate your production by spawning physical Seed Packs into your space, feeling every gain through satisfying haptic pulses and visual "Scale Pulses" that make the UI breathe. As your harvest grows, so does a mysterious, reactive "Eye" that looms over your progress, marking your journey toward the ultimate Prestige. It’s a satisfying loop of constant growth and strategic resets, where the goal isn't just to watch the numbers go up, but to feel the physical weight and mystical evolution of your expanding agricultural empire.
